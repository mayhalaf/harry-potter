Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

The film depicts the story of Harry Potter, as it unfolded in Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone,
Harry is a 10-year-old orphan boy who lives with his cruel relatives, the Dursleys.
On his 11th birthday, an oversized wizard appears, named Hagrid, who tells him that he is a wizard, the most famous in the wizarding world
because he managed to survive the murderous attack of the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort.
